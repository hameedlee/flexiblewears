Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept small or one-off orders?
Yes — we work with individuals as well as bulk clients. Pricing varies by volume; contact us for sample pricing.
Can I request sustainable materials?
Absolutely. We offer organic cotton, recycled polyester, and low-impact dyeing. We'll share material specs on request.
What is your turnaround time?
Standard production runs average 4–8 weeks depending on quantities and customization. Rush services are available for qualified orders.
